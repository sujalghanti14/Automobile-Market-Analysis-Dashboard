# 🚗 Automobile Market Analysis Dashboard  
**(2W & 4W | EV Trends | RTA Analysis | Zoho Analytics)**

---

## 📊 Overview

This project showcases a comprehensive **Automobile Market Intelligence Dashboard** built using **Zoho Analytics**. It analyzes large-scale vehicle registration data to uncover insights into market trends, EV adoption, and regional performance.

The dashboards are designed to support **business decision-making, market research, and competitive analysis** in the automobile sector.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze **2-wheeler and 4-wheeler market trends**
- Track **EV adoption across regions**
- Compare **brand and model performance**
- Identify **high-performing RTAs (regional markets)**
- Monitor **month-on-month sales trends**
- Evaluate **dealer performance (Raam vs Others)**

---

## 🧩 Dashboards Included

### 🛵 Ather (EV 2W Analysis)
- EV scooter registrations and trends  
- Market share vs competitors (TVS, Ola, Bajaj)  
- RTA-wise EV distribution  
- Month-on-month EV sales comparison  

---

### 🏍️ Honda (2W Market Analysis)
- Total 2W registrations (bikes vs scooters)  
- Brand comparison (Hero, TVS, Bajaj, Suzuki)  
- Regional (RTA-wise) performance  
- Monthly sales trends  

---

### 🚗 MG (4W + EV Analysis)
- EV vs non-EV performance  
- Model-wise insights (Comet, ZS-EV, Hector, etc.)  
- Dealer comparison (Raam vs Others)  
- RTA-level analysis  

---

### 🚘 Mercedes-Benz & BMW (Luxury Segment)
- Luxury car market share  
- EV penetration in premium segment  
- Brand comparison (MB vs BMW vs JLR)  
- Segment-wise and regional analysis  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- ⚡ Rapid growth in **EV adoption**, especially in 2W segment  
- 🛵 Ather shows strong performance in specific RTAs  
- 🏍️ Honda dominates the traditional 2W market  
- 🚗 MG leads in EV adoption within the 4W segment  
- 💎 Luxury EV adoption is emerging but still niche
